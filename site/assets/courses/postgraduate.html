We offer various courses for postgraduate students, ranging from master's degree and up to PhD's, students who have finished their bachelor degree in AHR will be able to immediately start they higher master's degree without having to go throw the whole registration process again

List of available courses:
  • Master's in Economics
  • PhD in Engineering(civil)
  • PhD in Engineering(manufacturing)
  • Master of Medical Sciences
  • PhD in Political Science
  • Master of Management(MMGT
  • Master in Nursing Science (Research)
  • Master of Business Administration(MBA)

the registration process is same as undergraduates, however postgraduate students will be able to define the research they are going to conduct based on the information provided, they'll also be able to choose the degree period.
