As an international University its part of our job to give students a place to stay and live in until they finish their studies, our mahallas(dorms) offer the students a place to go back to after a long day of classes and exhaustion, In each Mahallah you'll find small shops ,cafeterias laundry services and other necessary life needs.

We made sure the experience of living in AHR is same across all dorms, by consistently improving all places and giving the students an online platform to report any issues regarding in any of the Mahallah's facilities, each dorm is named after an old civilization to emphasize our strong connection with our past history.

We have a total of 6 dorms 3 for males and 3 for females:

Male Dorms:
  • Assyria: a major Semitic-speaking Mesopotamian of the ancient Near East and the Levant
  • Babylon: a key kingdom in ancient Mesopotamia from the 18th to 6th centuries BC
  • Canaan: a Semitic-speaking region in the Ancient Near East during the late 2nd millennium BC
Female Dorms:
  • Lapita: a prehistoric Pacific Ocean people who flourished in the Pacific Islands from about 1600 BCE to about 500 BCE
  • Maya: a Mesoamerican civilization noted for its hieroglyphic script—the only known fully developed writing system of the pre-Columbian Americas
  • Nanda: an ancient Indian dynasty that originated in Magadha region during the 4th century BCE and lasted between 345–321 BCE.

We wish you a great an enjoyable stay in AHR history dorms.
